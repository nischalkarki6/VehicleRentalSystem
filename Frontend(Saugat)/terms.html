Rental Terms & Conditions

Please review our mobility protocols for Nepal operations.

Rental Policy

  • The rental period begins at the time of vehicle pickup and ends at the specified return time.
  • CarCodemandu operates on a 24-hour cycle. Any vehicle returned more than 60 minutes late will incur a full day's rental charge.
  • Fuel must be returned at the same level as provided at the start of the rental. A refueling service fee plus fuel cost applies if returned lower.

Insurance & Liability

Comprehensive Coverage

All vehicles are covered by third-party liability insurance as per Nepal Road Safety Regulations.

Excess Amount

A maximum liability (excess) of NPR 50,000 applies to accidental damages.

Off-road misuse voids insurance coverage.

Driving Requirements (Nepal)

  • Minimum Age: 18 Years for economy; 25 Years for luxury fleet.
  • Valid Nepali Driving License or International Driving Permit (IDP).
  • Mandatory Security Deposit: NPR 15,000 (Fully Refundable).

Cancellation Policy

Free Cancellation

Up to 24 hours before pickup time.

Cancellation Fee

50% charge if within 24 hours of pickup.
